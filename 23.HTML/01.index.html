Narendra Damodardas Modi (Gujarati (listen); born 17 September 1950)[b] is an Indian politician serving as the 14th and cu rrent prime minister of India since 2014. Modi was the chief minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014 and is the Member of Parliament from Varanasi. He is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS), a right-wing Hindu nationalist paramilitary volunteer organisation He is the longest serving prime minister from outside the Indian National Congress.
